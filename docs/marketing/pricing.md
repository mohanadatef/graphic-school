# Pricing Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the pricing strategy for Graphic School 2.0, including pricing models, plans, and value justification.

## Pricing Philosophy

### Principles

1. **Transparent Pricing:** Clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees
2. **Value-Based:** Pricing reflects value delivered
3. **Scalable:** Plans that grow with customers
4. **Affordable:** Accessible to mid-market institutions
5. **Fair:** Competitive and fair pricing

## Pricing Models

### Subscription Model

**Monthly Subscription:**
- Recurring monthly billing
- Cancel anytime
- No long-term commitment
- Predictable costs

**Annual Subscription:**
- 20% discount vs. monthly
- Annual billing
- Better value
- Commitment discount

### Usage-Based Add-ons

**Additional Features:**
- Extra storage
- Additional users
- Premium support
- Custom integrations

**Includes:**

- Up to 100 students

- Small training centers
